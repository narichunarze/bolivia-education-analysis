# Bolivia Education System Analysis (2011–2024)

## Overview
Data analysis project exploring educational outcomes across Bolivia's
9 departments, with focus on the public vs private school gap.

Inspired by UNICEF's Programme Guidance for the Second Decade:
Programming with and for Adolescents certification.

## Key Findings
- 9 in 10 Bolivian students attend public school (stable since 2011)
- Public school dropout rate is 2x higher than private (~2.0% vs ~0.9%)
- 4-percentage-point gap in pass rates that hasn't closed in 10 years
- All 9 departments improved dropout rates between 2012 and 2024
- Male dropout rate consistently higher than female throughout the period

## Data Source
Instituto Nacional de Estadística (INE) — Bolivia
Cuadros estadísticos 3.02.02.01 – 3.02.02.05
